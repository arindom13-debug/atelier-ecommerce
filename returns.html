Returns & Exchanges.

Our policy

We want you to love what you receive. If something isn't right, we accept returns and exchanges within 30 days of delivery for unworn, unwashed items in their original condition with all tags attached.

Items marked as Final Sale, made-to-order pieces, and intimate garments are not eligible for return.

How to return

1

Email us at returns@atelier.co with your order number and the item(s) you wish to return.

2

We will send you a prepaid return label within 24 hours.

3

Pack the item carefully — ideally in the original tissue and box — and drop it with your chosen courier.

4

Once received and inspected, your refund will be processed within 5–7 business days to your original payment method.

Exchanges

To exchange for a different size or colour, follow the same return process and note the item you'd like instead. We'll hold it for you and ship as soon as we receive your return.

"We make very few pieces. If the size you want has sold out, we will place you first on the waiting list for the next batch."

Questions

Write to hello@atelier.co or visit us at our Dhaka or London ateliers. We respond to every message personally.
