The Craft

Every piece is hand-loomed by artisans in Dhaka and Rajshahi, Bangladesh. We work directly with master weavers, paying above fair-trade rates and producing in batches of no more than forty pieces per pattern. The process is intentionally slow — it takes a weaver three days to produce enough fabric for a single garment.
Our colours come from plant, bark, and mineral sources — indigo, turmeric, iron, tannin. No synthetic dyes enter our process. This means slight variation between pieces and a colour that deepens and softens over time rather than fading uniformly. These variations are not imperfections; they are the signature of the hand.

Sizing & Fit

Our pieces are designed with a relaxed, layered fit in mind. Most styles run true to size. Detailed measurements and a size guide are included on every product page. If you are between sizes, we generally recommend sizing up for drapier pieces and true-to-size for structured cuts.
