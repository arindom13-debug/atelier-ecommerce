Our Craft

Made slowly.
Made once.

Each piece begins at the loom. It takes one of our weavers three full days to produce enough fabric for a single garment — a pace we have never tried to accelerate. At Atelier, slowness is not a constraint. It is the work itself, made visible in every thread.

Heritage

Fifty years of quiet making.

Our founders arrived in Dhaka with a single belief: that enduring things demand time. Since 1975, we have worked with the same families of weavers, passing techniques not through manuals, but through hands. The knowledge lives in the fabric. You carry it with you.
